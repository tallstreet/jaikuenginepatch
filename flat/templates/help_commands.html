{{POST_NAME}}s and Comments

To your stream

Just type a message, it will be added as your latest post

To a channel

Start your message with #channelname

Example: #example Hello everyone!

Comments - CURRENTLY IM ONLY

Use @username to add a comment to the latest update from that user.

Example: @lordi Rock!

You can post to channels using the #channelname prefix also from other {{SITE_NAME}} interfaces, including the Web and desktop and mobile clients.

Commands

sign in <screenname> <password> - CURRENTLY IM ONLY

Sign in with your {{SITE_NAME}} account credentials to start posting and receiving updates

start

Activates updates

stop

Stops updates

help

Returns help message

follow username / #channelname

Adds the {{SITE_NAME}} member or channel to your contacts

leave username / #channelname

Removes the {{SITE_NAME}} member or channel from your contacts

sign out - CURRENTLY IM ONLY

Sign out of your current {{SITE_NAME}} account so you can log in with a different user

delete me

Deletes your {{SITE_NAME}} account

The following synonyms are also supported: "on, wake" for start; "end, quit, off, unsubscribe, pause" for stop; "add, join, F" for follow; and "remove, L" for leave. The commands are not case sensitive.
